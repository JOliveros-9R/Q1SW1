MY FAVORITE VIDEO GAME
WAR THUNDER

War Thunder is a free-to-play, cross-platform and a MMO military game. You can play in major battles on land, in the air and at sea, fighting with millions of other players from all over the world in an ever-evolving environment in a realistic competitive battles. There are over 2,500 highly detailed aircraft, helicopters, ground vehicles, warships and other combat vehicles with maps from different historical places.

This mode offers a more authentic and challenging gameplay experience for players that experienced. offer a more authentic and challenging gameplay experience.
Simulator Battles
This mode offers the most realistic experience (only for air battles) which requries you to have a joystick or gamepad.
